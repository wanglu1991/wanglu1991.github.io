Research Experience

Research on GPU analytical performance model

  • Investigating different performance behavior of memory divergent and non-memory divergent GPU application
  • Proposed a memory divergence model (MDM)
  • Our MDM model significantly increase prediction accuracy compared to state-of-art GPU analytical model
    • Dec. 2017 - Now

    Research on NoC congestion problem in GPUs

  • Observed inter-CTA locality in GPU applications
  • Proposed a intra-cluster colasecing (ICC) unit to track and colaesce misses from different SMs in a cluster to reduce NoC pressure
  • Analyzed interaction between different CTA scheduling policy and ICC unit
    • Sep. 2016 - Dec. 2017

    Research on fault-tolerance in Networks-on-Chip (NoC)

  • Fault tolerant routing algorithm research on NoC
  • Reliable NoC router design
